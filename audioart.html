Audio & Art

Art and Audio aren't neccesarily trades that I pride myself on, but over the years I have gotten much better as a creative. I have slowly become proficient in most Adobe applications over the past few years, which led to the work you will see below. All the work done on this page was done in Photoshop, Illustrator, XD, Premiere, or Audition. Enjoy!

This is actually a quick sketch I made for this exact website on Adobe XD. I showed where I wanted the navigation menu to be as well as the content of the page.

As my idea progressed so did my sketch. As you can see ive added a header in addition to a more in depth navigation menu. As I actually built the website my idea changed of course but these two sketches helped lay out the foundation of my website!

Moving away from sketches I have also created some UI for games. This is UI for a anime themed tower defense game my class was building. I tried making the assets as obnoxious as I possibly could since a lot of anime is very bright and colorful. This was all made in Adobe Illustrator.

LiikeSam · Narrative Mixdown

For this audio project I was tasked with creating a story by simply using sound effects. So I created just that! See if you can figure out my story!

LiikeSam · MIXINGaSONG

This project was a little difficult. I was given a folder full of different audio files and from that folder I needed to make a song out of it. I lifted the vocals and added all the instruments, but all of the files had issues (Too quiet, mixed poorly, weird effects, etc.). I fixed it as best I could and thats the audio you hear!

LiikeSam · Mood

For this last bit of audio I was tasked with mixing a song of my choosing to create three different moods. For the first mood I tried to make it sound like you were listening to the song from another room. For the second mood I tried to make it eerie sounding by lowering the beat and messing with the EQ of the vocals. For the last mood I went for a muffled sound. I hope you enjoy!
